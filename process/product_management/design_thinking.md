# Design Thinking

It is a process that allows a group of individuals to harness the concept around a project in order for it to be realized.

The steps involved in this process are :
1. Gather user input
2. Assemble notes
3. Identify Pain Points
4. Create Goals
5. Metrics
6. Build Personas
7. Ideation
8. Paper Prototypes
9. Scenarios
10. Usability
11. Story Maps
